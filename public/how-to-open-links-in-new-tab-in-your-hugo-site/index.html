How to Open Links in New Tab in Your Hugo Site

02 Nov, 2024

I wanted hugo to open external links in new tabs but after several trials, I failed. What I tried are:

  1. HTML
1<a href="https://example.com/" target="_blank">This is an example!</a>
  1. kramdown syntax
1[Example](https://www.example.com){:target="_blank"}

After that I was like:

Bruh, what happened?!!

And then, I find this which saves my life. I’ll just show how to do it directly, you can go to learn more if you have interest.

  1. Create render-link.html in ../layouts/_default/_markup.

  2. Copy and paste in it.

1{{/*  To tell hugo to open links in a new tab and do not let the site you are linking to to know the traffic came from your site  */}}
2<a href="{{ .Destination | safeURL }}"{{ with .Title}} title="{{ . }}"{{ end }}{{ if strings.HasPrefix .Destination "http" }} target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener"{{ end }}>
3    {{ .Text }}
4  </a>
  1. All finished, just build your site and enjoy.
